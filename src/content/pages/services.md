# Primary Care Services at Forward Family Medicine

## Hero

**Comprehensive primary care — all included in your membership.**

Forward Family Medicine provides the full breadth of family medicine, from your annual physical to complex chronic disease management. All included services are covered by your flat monthly membership fee. No copays. No billing surprises.

---

## Preventive Care & Wellness

Prevention is at the heart of what we do. We follow evidence-based USPSTF guidelines and work proactively to help you avoid chronic disease before it starts.

- **Annual physical exams** (comprehensive history, physical, and lab review)
- **Well-woman exams** (Pap smears, breast health, reproductive health)
- **Well-child visits and pediatric care** (newborn through adolescence)
- **Preventive health screenings** (blood pressure, cholesterol, diabetes, colorectal cancer, STI screening, and more)
- **Immunizations** (adult and pediatric vaccines, flu shots, travel vaccines — at cost)
- **Periodic health reviews** — proactive check-ins between annual visits
- **Health risk assessments** — personalized prevention planning based on family history and risk factors
- **Advance care planning** — goals of care, advance directives

---

## Acute & Urgent Care

When you're sick or injured, you need your doctor — not a walk-in clinic with no knowledge of your history. Members receive same-day or next-day access for acute concerns.

- **Respiratory infections** (colds, flu, bronchitis, sinusitis, COVID)
- **Ear infections and sore throats** (strep testing and treatment)
- **Urinary tract infections** (UTIs)
- **Skin conditions** (rashes, infections, wound care)
- **Minor injuries** (sprains, strains, lacerations)
- **Gastrointestinal illnesses** (nausea, diarrhea, stomach pain)
- **Allergic reactions** (mild to moderate)
- **Eye and ear concerns** (pink eye, ear pain)
- **Urgent medication needs**

> **Note:** For life-threatening emergencies, always call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. We handle urgent-but-not-emergency concerns that would otherwise send you to an urgent care center or ED.

## Chronic Disease Management

Chronic disease management thrives in the DPC model — because managing a condition well requires time, continuity, and a physician who knows your full history. All chronic disease management visits are included in your membership.

- **Type 2 diabetes and prediabetes** — A1C monitoring, medication management, lifestyle counseling
- **Hypertension (high blood pressure)** — medication management, dietary counseling, monitoring
- **Hyperlipidemia (high cholesterol)** — statin and non-statin management, lifestyle intervention
- **Hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism** — lab monitoring, medication titration
- **Asthma and COPD** — inhaler management, action plans, monitoring
- **Obesity and metabolic syndrome** — comprehensive, evidence-based weight management
- **Osteoporosis and bone health** — screening, supplementation, bisphosphonate management
- **Anxiety and depression** — medication management, behavioral health support, referrals
- **ADHD** — adult ADHD evaluation and medication management (age/scope dependent — confirm with practice)
- **Autoimmune conditions** — co-management with specialists
- **Chronic pain** — multimodal management, coordination with pain specialists

## Lab & Diagnostic Testing

Laboratory work is ordered through our wholesale lab partnerships — meaning you pay near-cost pricing, not inflated billed rates. Common labs cost a fraction of what you'd pay through insurance.

**Example savings (approximate):**

| Test | Typical insured rate | Forward FM member rate |
|---|---|---|
| Complete metabolic panel | $40–$100+ | $6–$10 |
| Lipid panel | $30–$80+ | $4–$7 |
| HbA1c | $25–$60+ | $4–$6 |
| Thyroid panel (TSH) | $40–$100+ | $8–$12 |
| Complete blood count | $25–$60+ | $3–$5 |

*Rates are approximate and subject to change. Imaging and advanced diagnostics are not included in membership — we help you navigate these efficiently.*

## Services Not Included in Membership

The following are not included in DPC membership and would be billed separately through your insurance or paid out-of-pocket:

- Emergency room care and hospitalization
- Specialist visits and procedures
- Imaging (X-ray, MRI, CT, ultrasound) — we assist with ordering and finding cost-effective options
- Physical therapy, occupational therapy, chiropractic
- Dental and vision

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do you see children?**
Yes. We provide pediatric care from newborns through adolescence. A child membership can be added to a parent's family plan. [See membership details →](/membership)

**Are lab fees included in membership?**
Lab fees are separate but priced at wholesale/near-cost. You will always pay far less than standard billed rates.

**Do you prescribe controlled substances?**
We prescribe controlled substances for appropriate clinical indications (e.g., ADHD medication, pain management in limited circumstances) in accordance with applicable laws and clinical guidelines.

**What if I need imaging?**
We order imaging and work to find cost-effective options for our members. We can direct you to facilities with transparent, competitive cash pricing when insurance does not cover it.

**What's the best way to know if you can help with my specific condition?**
[Schedule a free Meet & Greet →](/become-a-patient) and ask us directly. We are happy to tell you what falls within our scope and how we'd approach your care.
