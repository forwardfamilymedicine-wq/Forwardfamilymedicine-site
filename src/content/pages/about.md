# About Forward Family Medicine

## Hero

**Medicine the way it was always meant to be practiced.**

Forward Family Medicine was built on a simple conviction: primary care works best when a doctor has time to truly know the people they care for. That belief shapes everything we do — from how we schedule appointments to how we structure our practice.

---

## Our Story

Dr. Bigley and Dr. Lieberman practiced traditional family medicine for [X] years and watched, year after year, as administrative burden and insurance constraints squeezed the time available for patients. A 15-minute appointment slot was never enough to do right by someone with diabetes, a new diagnosis, or a child who needed more than a quick look.

The Direct Primary Care model offered a different path — one where the doctor-patient relationship, not the billing code, is the center of care. Forward Family Medicine opened in Wayne, PA as a membership-based practice to give Main Line families exactly that: a physician who is genuinely available, genuinely present, and genuinely invested in their health.

## Our Approach to Care

### Relationship First
We keep our patient panel small by design. That means your doctor recognizes your voice on the phone, remembers what you talked about last visit, and has read your chart before you walk in the door.

### Whole-Person Medicine
We look beyond the chief complaint to understand what's happening in your life. Stress, sleep, nutrition, and relationships all affect your health, and we address them as part of your care plan.

### Lifestyle Medicine Integration
As a lifestyle medicine-informed practice, we use evidence-based behavioral interventions — nutrition counseling, physical activity guidance, sleep optimization, stress management — as first-line tools, not afterthoughts. [Learn more about our lifestyle medicine approach →](/lifestyle-medicine)

### Unhurried, Thoughtful Visits
Appointments are scheduled to fit your needs — not a billing clock. Whether you need 15 minutes for a quick concern or 60 minutes to review lab results and build a chronic disease management plan, we have time for you.

## Our Practice Philosophy in Three Sentences

We believe primary care should feel like having a trusted physician in the family. We believe prevention is more powerful than treatment. And we believe that when patients and doctors have time to build a real relationship, health outcomes improve — for everyone.

We are proud to be part of the Wayne community and to serve families across the Main Line.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Forward Family Medicine accepting new patients?**
Yes — we are currently accepting new members. We intentionally keep our panel small, so we encourage you to reach out before we reach capacity. [Schedule a free Meet & Greet →](/become-a-patient)

**Do you see children and adults?**
Yes. We care for patients of all ages, from newborns and children to adults and seniors.

**What if my needs are complex?**
The DPC model is well suited to complex, chronic cases because we have the time to manage them properly. We also coordinate with specialists and facilitate referrals when needed.

**Are you affiliated with a hospital?**
We maintain admitting privileges and referral relationships with local hospitals and specialists. [Contact us](/become-a-patient) for details on our hospital affiliations.
