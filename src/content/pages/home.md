## How Membership Works

Forward Family Medicine runs on a simple model: you pay one flat monthly fee, and we provide all the primary care you need. No insurance billing. No per-visit charges. No surprise costs.

| What you get | What you skip |
|---|---|
| Unlimited visits | Copays and deductibles |
| Direct doctor access | Long hold times |

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Direct Primary Care the same as concierge medicine?**
They're closely related. Both offer smaller patient panels, direct doctor access, and longer visits. DPC typically uses a flat monthly membership and does not bill insurance for primary care. [Learn more about DPC →](/direct-primary-care)

**Do I still need health insurance with a DPC membership?**
We recommend keeping a catastrophic or high-deductible plan for hospitalizations, specialist care, and emergencies. Your DPC membership covers primary care — often at a much lower total cost than traditional insurance copays and deductibles alone.

**How quickly can I get an appointment?**
Members typically get same-day or next-day appointments. Many questions can be handled the same day by phone or message without coming in at all.

**Can you manage my chronic conditions?**
Yes. Chronic disease management is a core part of what we do. Conditions like diabetes, hypertension, thyroid disorders, and high cholesterol are well-suited to the DPC model because ongoing care is fully included in your membership.
