# Membership at Forward Family Medicine

## Hero

**One simple monthly fee. Unlimited primary care. No surprises.**

Membership at Forward Family Medicine gives you direct access to a physician who knows you, answers when you call, and has time to help you get and stay well — for a flat monthly fee that's typically less than what most people pay in insurance copays and deductibles each year.

---

## What Membership Includes

Your monthly membership covers the full spectrum of primary care:

### Visits & Access
- Unlimited in-office visits — no per-visit fee, ever
- Same-day and next-day appointments for acute concerns
- Telehealth visits (video and phone) included
- Direct messaging, texting, and phone access to your doctor
- After-hours urgent questions handled by your own physician (not an answering service)

### Preventive & Chronic Care
- Annual physical exams and wellness visits
- Preventive health screenings (per USPSTF guidelines)
- Immunizations (at cost)
- Chronic disease management — diabetes, hypertension, thyroid, asthma, cholesterol, and more
- Medication management and refills

### Lifestyle Medicine
- Personalized nutrition, movement, sleep, and stress counseling
- Health coaching integrated into your care plan
- Behavioral health support and care coordination
- [Learn more about lifestyle medicine →](/lifestyle-medicine)

### Cost-Saving Benefits
- Lab work at wholesale/near-cost pricing (significant savings vs. billed rates)
- Prescription medications at cost through our direct dispensing partnerships
- Specialist referral coordination — we advocate for you
- No insurance billing overhead = savings passed to you

## How Membership Works {#how-it-works}

### Step 1 — Schedule a Free Meet & Greet
A 20-minute, no-obligation conversation with Dr. Bigley and Dr. Lieberman to ask questions, tour the practice, and decide if Forward Family Medicine is right for you.

### Step 2 — Enroll & Complete Your Health History
Enrollment takes about 10 minutes online. You'll complete a health history form and we'll request records from your previous provider.

### Step 3 — Schedule Your First Comprehensive Visit
New members receive a thorough onboarding visit — typically 45–60 minutes — where we review your full health history, establish your care goals, and build a personalized prevention plan.

### Step 4 — You Have Direct Access to Your Doctor
From day one, you can reach Dr. Bigley and Dr. Lieberman directly by phone, text, or message — and schedule same-day or next-day appointments whenever you need care.

## Membership vs. Traditional Insurance: A Comparison

| | Forward Family Medicine | Traditional Insurance |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly cost | Flat membership fee | Premiums + copays + deductibles |
| Visit time | 30–60 min typical | 7–15 min typical |
| Doctor access | Direct (phone/text/message) | Via scheduling + call center |
| Wait for appointment | Same or next day | Days to weeks |
| Lab costs | Wholesale (near cost) | Billed at insurance rates |
| Annual total (est.) | Predictable | Unpredictable |

> **Important:** DPC membership is **not** a replacement for health insurance. We strongly recommend maintaining a high-deductible health plan (HDHP) or health-sharing plan for hospital, specialist, and emergency coverage. Many members find their total healthcare spend decreases significantly when they combine DPC membership with an HDHP.

## Is Membership Tax-Deductible?

DPC membership fees may be deductible as medical expenses under IRS guidelines if you itemize deductions, and may be eligible for use with a Health Savings Account (HSA) in some circumstances. Tax laws change — please consult your tax advisor for guidance specific to your situation.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is there a long-term commitment?**
We offer both month-to-month and annual membership options. Annual plans include a modest discount.

**What happens if I need to see a specialist?**
We coordinate specialist referrals for you and stay in communication with consulting physicians. Specialist visits and procedures are billed separately through your insurance.

**Can I use my HSA or FSA?**
Many of our members use HSA or FSA funds for eligible expenses (labs, medications, procedures). The deductibility of membership fees as HSA-qualified expenses is subject to federal law and your specific plan — consult your benefits administrator.

**What if I need care while traveling?**
You can reach Dr. Bigley and Dr. Lieberman by phone or message no matter where you are. For emergencies, always go to the nearest emergency room.

**Is this available for employer groups?**
Yes. We work with small and medium-sized employers to offer DPC memberships as a benefit. [Contact us for employer pricing →](/become-a-patient#employer)

**How do I cancel?**
Members may cancel with 30 days' written notice. There are no long-term contracts required for our month-to-month plan.
