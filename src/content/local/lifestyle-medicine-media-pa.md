# Lifestyle Medicine Near Media, PA

## About Media, PA

Media's community-oriented character attracts residents who value quality of life and think carefully about health and well-being. Forward Family Medicine's lifestyle medicine approach speaks directly to that orientation: using evidence-based behavioral interventions to address the conditions that most affect long-term health — metabolic disease, cardiovascular risk, stress, and sleep dysfunction — rather than waiting to treat them after they cause problems. The practice is 12 miles north in Wayne.

## Prevention-Centered Care, Accessible from Media

Forward Family Medicine integrates lifestyle medicine into every DPC membership. Nutrition, activity, sleep, stress management, and chronic disease prevention are included as clinical standards — not referrals to outside programs. Telehealth makes most lifestyle consultations available to Media patients without requiring a drive to Wayne.

Dr. Lieberman is dual board-eligible in Family Medicine and Lifestyle Medicine — the only such physician at any DPC practice on the Main Line.
